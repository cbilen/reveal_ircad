##  Spectral Features

For a given graph, $\mathit{G}$, with **weight matrix**, $\mathbf{W}$, we can compute the graph laplacian as:

``$\mathbf{L} = \mathbf{D} - \mathbf{W} \qquad \text{where }\mathbf{D} \triangleq \text{diag}(d_i),\quad d_i \triangleq \sum_j w_{ij}$``
